## Root Cause
The user needed to know the specific technical steps and tools required to reliably capture terminal block-character animated QR code output into an MP4 file, particularly to ensure compatibility with `qr_decoder_lite.py`.

## Solution
Researched and tested terminal capturing techniques. The most reliable solution for animated QR codes is a direct screen capture using FFmpeg and `x11grab`.
Created a comprehensive documentation guide located at `docs/capture_qrcode_to_mp4.md` detailing the prerequisites, step-by-step instructions, and headless automation script examples to capture the QR codes generated by `qr_encoder_lite.py` into an MP4 format.
